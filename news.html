Off Piste Goes Green

Sustainability at the Heart of the Festival

Off Piste Music Festival is not just about the music; it's about leaving a positive impact on the environment. This year, the organizers are proud to announce their commitment to sustainability. From banning single-use plastics to eco-friendly shuttles, Off Piste is taking bold steps to minimize its carbon footprint. Join us in dancing responsibly and preserving the beauty of our mountain haven.
